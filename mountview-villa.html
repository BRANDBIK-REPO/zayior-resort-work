Intro

Welcome to Mountview Villa at Zayior Resort

Nestled atop a scenic hill, Mountview Villa at Zayior Resort offers an exclusive retreat with breathtaking views of the surrounding mountains and lush forests. Located within 32 hectares of pristine land in Kakkadampoyil, the villa provides a tranquil, elevated escape with panoramic vistas that stretch across the vast landscape. Surrounded by nature, this serene villa is perfect for those looking to experience the beauty of the mountains, the comfort of modern amenities, and the peace of seclusion.

Indulge in the ultimate luxury getaway at the Bubble House, where breathtaking views, unparalleled privacy, and eco-friendly design come together to create an unforgettable experience. Whether you're seeking romance, adventure, or simply a chance to reconnect with nature, this exclusive retreat promises to elevate your stay to new heights of serenity and sophistication.

Hotel's Rules

Take into account the hotel's rules

  • No Smoking Policy

Smoking is prohibited inside the villa to ensure the comfort of all guests. Designated smoking areas are available outdoors for those who wish to smoke.

  • Noise Control

To preserve the tranquility of the resort, we request guests to keep noise levels to a minimum, particularly during the evening hours. Please be considerate of other guests and the natural surroundings.

  • Room Capacity

For your safety, always lock the villa doors when leaving or when inside. Do not share your room key with anyone, and immediately report any suspicious activity to resort staff.

  • Security Measures

For your safety and security, always keep your room door locked when inside or leaving the room. Do not share your room key or access codes with anyone outside your party, and report any suspicious activity to hotel staff immediately.
