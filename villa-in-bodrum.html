Hotel's Rules

Take into account the hotel's rules

  • No Smoking Policy

For the comfort and safety of all guests, smoking is strictly prohibited in the hotel rooms. Designated smoking areas are available outside the premises for those who wish to smoke.

  • Noise Control

Please be considerate of other guests and keep noise levels to a minimum, especially during late hours. Excessive noise can disrupt the peaceful atmosphere of the hotel and disturb the rest of fellow guests.

  • Room Capacity

Each room has a maximum occupancy limit based on fire safety regulations and room size. Please adhere to this limit to ensure the safety and comfort of all guests. Additional guests beyond the specified limit may not be permitted.

  • Security Measures

For your safety and security, always keep your room door locked when inside or leaving the room. Do not share your room key or access codes with anyone outside your party, and report any suspicious activity to hotel staff immediately.
